About Us

Alsa Lands Ltd is a real estate company that was established to offer value added and genuine land in Malindi and other prime areas in Kenya as well as the rest of East African Region.

While not conforming to the traditional real estate boundaries when it comes to buying, selling, renting and property management, we are always coming up with new and innovative ways to satisfy our client’s need and be the leading company in real estate industry.

We exist to ensure that our clients own property easily and rightfully, with a promise of great returns on the investment.

Our Values
  • Truth-We never bend the truth for us to achieve our sales, we make sure that we always provide the truth when it comes to the information of our properties.
  • Responsibility-We make sure that each member of our team is responsible enough to attend all the needs and inquiries of our clients in a professional way.
  • Understanding-We seek to understand the client’s needs, as this is the most important part of our customer service. By understand what our customers need, we can provide suggestions and recommendations to our clients.
